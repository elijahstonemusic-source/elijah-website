Bio

Elijah Stone is a New York–based singer-songwriter blending pop, rock, soul, and blues into a fresh yet timeless sound. His debut EP Twenty-Second Catch showcases groove-forward, hook-driven songs with candid lyricism, and his recent work includes a cinematic music video for his song, “Look Listen.” He is currently booking shows in New York City and the Northeast.
